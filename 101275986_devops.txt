COMP3104 DevOps
Course Description
In this course students will learn the procedures, protocols and best practices that a DevOps engineer brings to a workplace environment to increase productivity.
Analyze and evaluate various developer operation implementations available and implement hands-on work using industry standard toolkit.
Create solutions to complex business problems by adhering to the core responsibilities of DevOps.

Topics:
1 - Intro, BASH & The CLI
2 - Git, GitHub, & more BASH
3 - Version Control for DevOps
4 - Developer Operations Landscape
5 - Hosting High Geared Single Page Applications & Work tracking tools
6 - Continuous Integration / Continuous Deployment
7 - CI/CD Pipeline Sequences
8 - Working with Cloud Infrastructure
9 - Automating DevOps & Quality assurance
10 - DevOps Assembly Lines
11 - Commonly Used DevOps Tech Exposed
